About

Tiago Choupina is a musician and producer. He has several projects of his own and also with his band ¿ WHY NOT ? .

Tiago Choupina released his first official single, "Your Vibe" in 2020. Tiago was studying guitar at a music academy but after 4 years he left and continued as a self-taught person. He decided to deepen his knowledge in music production and also started playing keyboards.
